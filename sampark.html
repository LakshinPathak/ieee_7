“SAMPARK'24”

SAMPARK is an Annual Flagship Networking event of IEEE Gujarat Section which will be taking place on 25th February 2024, and will be attended by IEEE Student Branches of Universities as well as Professors and Young Professionals across Gujarat. The main aim of this event is to share the yearly plan of Gujarat Section of Executive committee along with networking and socializing amongst the IEEE student branches spread across all over Gujarat, in order to strengthen partnerships and produce fruitful collaborations and thus have a United plan of action for the upcoming year.
